A little about me

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering (EE) from the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB), Indonesia, graduating with the highest GPA in the EE class of 2024 (3.90/4.00). During my studies, I discovered my passion for Signal Processing, Machine Learning, and Embedded Systems. I demonstrated my commitment to sharing knowledge by serving as an academic assistant twice: once for a Digital Signal Processing class and once for an Engineering Electromagnetics class. Additionally, I have created over 100 hours of educational content, amassing 140k+ total views on my YouTube channel. I excel at bridging theoretical concepts with real-world applications, as evidenced by my track record as a lab practicum coordinator for the Electronics I & II laboratories.

Apart from my academic study, I participated in various leadership and organizational programs throughout my university studies that includes basic military training with the Indonesian National Army (TNI) and Resimen Mahasiswa (MenWa) Mahawarman YON I/ITB. I also contributed to the Student Union Executive Board (HME ITB) as the Minister of Education and Division Head of Human Development in WORKSHOP HME ITB.

My Specialties

Signal Processing

  • Digital Signal Processing:
    • MATLAB(Digital Signal Processing Toolbox) for analyzing and designing digital systems.
    • Audacity and other audio editing tools for processing and analyzing sound signals.
    • Python with libraries such as NumPy for implementing signal processing algorithms and data analysis.

  • Analog Signal Processing:
    • Working with analog-to-digital (A/D) signals in microcontrollers.
    • Analyzing and troubleshooting analog signals using oscilloscopes.
    • Circuit design and analog filter using RC network & Op-amp to optimize signal quality in real-world applications.

Machine Learning

  • Querying and managing databases using PostgreSQL.
  • Supervised learning (regression & classification) using scikit-learn + implementing from scratch using NumPy.
  • Deep learning model development and training using TensorFlow.
  • Digital image processing using OpenCV for machine vision tasks.

Embedded System

  • Developing IoT projects using the ESP32, programmed with the ESP-IDF framework and utilizing the HTTP/MQTT protocol, implemented with Node-RED for GUI-based interaction.
  • Embedded firmware programming with C language for various microcontrollers such as STM32 using STM32CubeIDE, and Arduino (Atmega32) using Arduino IDE.
  • Task scheduling and Real time operating system based on FreeRTOS kernel for uC.
  • Embedded system design on Infineon microcontrollers (e.g., CY8CPROTO-062-4343W) using Modus Toolbox.
  • Reading datasheets and interfacing various electronic components, sensors, and modules with microcontrollers, supporting communication protocols such as USB, RS232/UART, SPI, and I2C.
  • PCB Design using Ki-CAD and 3D design for casing using FreeCAD / SolidWorks

Passionate in Teaching

One of my strengths is my deep understanding of the theoretical concepts behind my subjects. I embrace the philosophy of learning through teaching and so passionate about sharing my knowledge with others. During my college years, I developed a YouTube channel that has produced over 100 hours of educational content on electrical engineering topics, including signals and systems, digital signal processing, engineering electromagnetics, the physics of electricity and magnetism, electronics, and other practical topics in electrical engineering. After graduating, I began developing my professional intellectual property (IP) called Berjibaku Prestasi to continue my teaching journey with a more professional production setup and methodologies.


Tuned for doing teamwork

Throughout my time at university, I was involved in various activites, both in student activities and laboratory. My involvement included participating in the Student Union (Himpunan Mahasiswa Elektroteknik), Workshop HME, and Resimen Mahasiswa (MenWa), as well as serving as a lab assistant and a lab practicum coordinator. These experiences taught me how to interact effectively with diverse personalities, show respect to others, delegate tasks efficiently, and provide proper support to ensure group tasks are completed successfully.


Hustle and Health Balance

Amidst my many activities, I always strive to maintain the trident of physical health: proper nutrition, regular exercise, and adequate rest. I aim for a minimum of six hours of sleep every day, consistently try to achieve 6,000 steps daily, and do my best to avoid sugar. I am also an active member of the IAE runners community, where I have completed six half marathons (21.1 km) and one 30 km race with pace faster than 5:30.
